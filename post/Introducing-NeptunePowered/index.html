Introducing NeptunePowered

Apr 18, 2015

What is NeptunePowered?

NeptunePowered is a team dedicated to making server owners lives easier.

What is Neptune?

Neptune is a work-in-progress implementation of CanaryLib for ‘Vanilla’ Minecraft. It will be implemented using Mixin. Neptune will also use mcp mappings.

Why?

I am a big supporter of Canary, and feel (in comparison to other server mods) Canary lacks a great implementation. This is why I am creating Neptune.

What about Arno?

I am not stopping development of Arno, infact Arno has been acquired by NeptunePowered.

What is Arno?

Arno is a similar product to Neptune, except from being implemented ontop Minecraft Forge.
